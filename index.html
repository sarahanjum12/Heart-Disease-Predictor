About Heart Disease Prediction

Heart disease is a leading cause of death globally. This website provides a tool to predict the likelihood of heart disease based on various factors such as age, gender,RestingECG, cholesterol levels, and more.This data set dates from 1988 and consists of four databases: Cleveland, Hungary, Switzerland, and Long Beach V but modified by adding additional instances through various sources to increase the accuracy of the model and provide better results.All the user inputs and predictions are stored in a database for optimizing the model for better performance.

You can learn more about heart disease and its risk factors from reputable sources like the American Heart Association and the CDC.
